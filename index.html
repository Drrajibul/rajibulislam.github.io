Career Bio:

Dr. Md. Rajibul Islam was born in a small town in the northern part of Bangladesh. He is currently working as an Associate Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Bangladesh University of Business and Technology (BUBT). He was previously an Assistant Professor and the Head of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Asia Pacific (UAP). In addition, he was a Visiting Ph.D. Researcher at the University of Limerick in Ireland as part of the Erasmus Mundus INTACT Program. He also worked as Research Assistant at the Photonics Research Centre, University of Malaya, Software Engineer at London English Academy (LEA), Research Assistant (RA) in the R&D at Clarify Consulting Sdn Bhd., and Research Assistant at the Ibnu Sina Institute for Fundamental Science Studies, University Technology Malaysia (UTM). Dr. Islam received his Ph.D. in Physics (Photonics Engineering) from the Photonics Research Centre, University of Malaya (UM), in 2017. He received his M.Sc. in IT (Biometric Image Processing) from Multimedia University, Malaysia (MMU) and Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) Degree from Indira Gandhi National Open University, New Delhi, India, in 2010 and 2004, respectively. His research interests include optical fiber sensors, fiber grating, few-mode fiber grating, fiber optic chemical sensors and biosensors, structure health monitoring, Machine learning, and Artificial Intelligence. He has over 75 peer-reviewed scientific publications in international journals and conferences, as well as books and book chapters. Dr. Islam volunteers as a reviewer of several international journals, and he is also a regular scientific committee member of several international conferences. He is a member of IAEng, IACSIT, ASTRC-I, MIR Labs, and the Optical Society of America (OSA).
